Space Pioneer

Costs:

Our first commercial space flights are planned for late 2024.

Booking:

Your booking is required 13 months prior to the flight date.

A non-refundable deposit of $15K is required as a downpayment for a space flight booking. This will be deducted from the final amount which you pay.

Purchase:

All payments are required to be made in full 12 months before the flight.

We accept all major credit cards and wire transfers.

All costs are listed in USD.

Trotter-5:

  • The Trotter-5 has a maximum carrying capacity of 5 guests excluding staff.

  • The maximum carrying capacity in pounds is 900, or 410 kgs.

  • Full spacecraft booking $1.0 million (Average cost of $200k per person).

    • Trotter-6:

  • The Trotter-6 has a maximum carrying capacity of 6 guests excluding staff.

  • The maximum carrying capacity in pounds is 1080, or 490 kgs.

  • Full spacecraft booking $1.2 million (Average cost of $200k per person).

    • Trotter-8:

  • The Trotter-8 has a maximum carrying capacity of 8 guests excluding staff.

  • The maximum carrying capacity in pounds is 1620, or 735 kgs.

  • Full spacecraft booking $2.0 million (Average cost of $250k per person).

    • Refunds:

    The deposit is non-refundable. The balance payment is 100% refundable until around 12 months prior to the planned flight.

    For cancellations 6 months prior to the trip, we refund 50% of the payment excluding the deposit. For cancellations less than 6 months prior to the trip there is no refund.

    After Your Deposit:

    After your deposit has been received, you are part of the Space Pioneer community. You will receive a welcome gift, regular updates via email and social media, and exclusive event invitations such as major milestone celebrations and behind-the-scenes tours of our facilities. In addition, you will have the opportunity to meet fellow Space Pioneers and build bonds to last a lifetime.
