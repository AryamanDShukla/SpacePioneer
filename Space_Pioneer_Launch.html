Space Pioneer

Launch:


The Day Prior:

There is nothing quite like this on Earth!

Evening prior to the launch we invite you to the exquisite dinner at our private Cocoa Beach resort.

Toast your departure with a glass of champagne with our team lead, Stuti Rao with her special guest team. Then ride into the horizon over to Cape Canaveral Space Force Station launch pad. Your team commander, Avir Shukla will be waiting for you.


Lift Off:

The countdown to liftoff begins with the flash of fire as the rocket engines ignite. Seconds later, the rocket soars overhead and the rumble of the engines becomes a roar that vibrates across the landscape. Witness the Earth fade behind you as you are lifted into the expanse of space ahead of you. The view will stay embedded for a lifetime.


Settle Into Your New Habitat:

Sit back and savor the details about your new environment. Today, you experience the Earth as an interconnected planet, a crucial part of our vast global biosphere. This unique experience of an expanded view of our universe will be the life-transforming perspective for you as a Space Pioneer.


Invite Your Loved Ones To The Experience:

Invite your friends and family to see you. Space Pioneer has incorporated a special visitor center adjoining Kennedy Space Center. At the Pioneer Center, your close ones can learn about the scientific experiments and artistic creations of Space Pioneer that make your journey most special and unique.
