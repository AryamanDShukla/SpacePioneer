Recovery:

However, the journey is not over once you land. Because of space's microgravity environment, your body's structure has weakened. You can limit this with exercise, but even with that, you will need a few weeks to months of physical therapy to make sure that your body can still correctly function after going through that microgravity environment for an extended period of time. At base, we have many licensed medical professionals and physical therapists that will be overlooking and ensuring that your recovery is as fast, safe, and reliable as possible.
